Bio

James William Buffett was born on December 25, 1946. He is best know for his islan escapism themed music. He has recorded hit songs such as "Margaritaville" and "Come Monday". He has a devoted fan base known as Parrotheads.

Aside from his career in music, Buffett is a best-selling writer and is involved in two restaurant chains named after two of his best-known songs, "Margaritaville" and "Cheesburger in Paradise".
